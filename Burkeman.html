4,000 Weeks Interview with Oliver Burkeman

As a first-semester journalism grad student, I interviewed Guardian columnist Oliver Burkeman about his most recent book 4,000 Weeks: Time Management for Mortals, which is an exploration on the nature of time and humanity's relationship with it over history.
